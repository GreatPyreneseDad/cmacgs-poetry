# Love is Why

Why  

Love is why  

wake up and breathe  

I made us coffee  

and last night  

I stayed up late  

Making a persimmon tart  

Roasted pecans and coconut  

It tasted so good  

Almost as good  

As it feels to lay next to you  

Long mornings never grow old  

Why  

Love is Why  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-11-08*
