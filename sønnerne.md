# Sønnerne

A war in our minds, set there like a seed of honor,  
to be grown to fruit and squeezed into the cup  
of those who spill for a living  

I took the pith and I'm weaving it into a net  
to hold those who will fall through their cracks  

A single source lights the whole world  

Shadows like homes for those who will be spilled  

To love is sin to breathe to live to find that place deep within  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2026-03-27*
