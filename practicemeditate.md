# Practice:Meditate

Practice and challenge yourself to meditate  

A buffer to death in any way  

mind candy that speeds up time  

focus concentrate bend your mind  

on a single state  

longer each and every day  

free tethered senses  

how sweet its breath  

to abate the rush of synthetic death  



skillets of wine are nothing fresh  

the pressures looming disctraction  

crushing in on peacefull factions  

to break the bonds of chemical actions  

3.5 billion years since lifes inception  

into and out of your perception  

floats an idea of decay  

the smell of roses your mind’s correction  

be it long or short in time  

the mind will mend and bend in line  

the theories of man in arts refined  

to crush his ambition on borrowed time.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2011-12-07*
