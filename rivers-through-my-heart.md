# Rivers through my heart

It’s a one way ticket  

This winding unstoppable force  

Eroding the banks of my long held beliefs  

The mountain can be found in the gravel  

Smooth pebbles once proud boulders  

Every color of the mountains being  

Held in two hands  

Feet sinking in to the cold water  

It’s a one way ticket I think  

And I’m glad it is  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2021-02-19*
