# Oyster

I wanted to see you  

But I cannot go past  

This picket fence  

So I swim  

Through the waves  

Down deep inside  

Reflections of you everywhere  

I am an oyster making a pearl  

From this painfully beautiful intrusion  

Its nacre essence perfect  

All colors from all places  

Can you see the beauty?  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-01-20*
