# Smaerd dreamS

Fugitive of reality  

Figure of mass affection  

Finding no solace in the cradle  

Of the masters of the universe  

Picture yourself inside their world  

Pawns or peon or something less  

Look, can you realize? We have built  

Our own bridle and yoke  

Choking on our own self Interests  

Do you even pin on Pinterest? Bro?  

That ominous clicking as we stare  

Into worlds of our minuscule desires.  

Click click click click click click click  

Like that, like this, comment here  

Subscribe, follow, friend, unfriend  

Block.  

We have gone from blue screen death  

To green screen dreams  

What is truth and where is reality?  

Are we all product of technological depravity?  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-08-25*
