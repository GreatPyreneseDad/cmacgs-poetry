# A Subtle Afternoon

How bright these stars become  

When we turn off the lights  

Is the same true for love?  

A lit room on a dark night  

A sunrise in a cloudy sky  

Subtlety has it’s virtue  

Like god wispering in your ear  

Through the wind and trees  

Or that tingling feeling you get  

When you really feel something  

Connection to the eternal  

Maybe just warmth from a Sun  

The light through leaves  

Never ceases to amaze me  

Like the milky way on a beach  

Like the first late fall breeze  

A chill, a sentiment, a soft cheek  

A morning I love you  

A subtle afternoon  

And all you need is you  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-11-16*
