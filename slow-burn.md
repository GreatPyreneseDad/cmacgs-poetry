# Slow Burn

The slow burn  

Beating drums betray you  

Your heart; thump thump  

Always tripping on the hook  

Beyond dreams; the look it hurts  

5th dimension mentioned  

You are stuck on earth  

Far from father; far from her  

Simple syrup; simple birth  

Crying baby; crying curse  

Not accepted; rejection hurts  

Tears from her temple; slow burn  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-09-30*
