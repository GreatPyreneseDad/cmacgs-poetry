# Will of the Jade

One cannot will a stone to be there  

It is just there or it isn’t  

In your mind it’s quantum  

This binary world  

Where this choice or that causes  

Storms to rage out of control  

One cannot will love or happiness  

They are either there or they are not  

In your mind you can pretend  

But in your heart you will know truth  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2017-01-03*
