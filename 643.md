# Jade Tears 

If I knew you were going to cry  

I would have never even smiled  

That day you walked into my home  

Dancing on the kitchen floor  

Of course I remember  

Leaning in I thought  

This is my moment in time  

Let this be the begining  

Or let me end  

10 kilometers is too short  

of a warm up  

Big Sur is where I will find  

That heart that i forgot  

My one and only  

Jade cove is mine  

One day i will carve my place  

In her glowing green shorelines  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-12*
