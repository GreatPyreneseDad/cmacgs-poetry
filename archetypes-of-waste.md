# Archetypes of Waste

Sorry soulless flesh bags  

Wasting away on the shores  

Of Lake Michigan  

On the shores of life  

Shrug your shoulders at suffering  

Think to yourself I’m glad it’s not me  

Can you even feel your own  

Heartbeat  

Can you even feel anything but your own  

Desires  

I’m tired of this wasteland  

This spiritual dump  

In the heart of Gods country  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-07-03*
