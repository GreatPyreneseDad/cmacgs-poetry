# Inside the 24x

in the presence of many one feels lost  

more a stranger now than when alone  

the landscape screaming in the opposite direction  

my concentration fades from those around me  

swimming in a sea of voices  

none more penetrating than my own  

i build the walls around my own voice  

echos through empty halls  

short power full intervals of distraction  

weigh down on the shelter of inner happenings  

a broken truce their faces recorded  

inhabit the thoughts of an unoccupied mind  

each its own castle of removal, isolation  

sensory barriers complement small social shields  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-01-20*
