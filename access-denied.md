# Access Denied

Check points choke the health care  

To be verified to go anywhere  

100  meters away children die at 3 to 5 times the rate.  

If you live in Gaza, where the grass is dying.  

Are the Israeli’s lieing on the otherside of the wall.  

Westbank is walled in.  

The humanitarian aid is walled out.  

Hague convention Article 46 says  

The people who sign this convention  

They must help the territory they are occupying.  

Check points choke our rights  

At night, In a set up firefight  

My father was shot in the right  

The right spot, he said  

He is so glad it wasn’t his head  

He could be dead, If his ID had not been read.  

Chery red white t-shirt  

So beautiful the color  

Blood stained seat belts  

While shouting voices echo  

These people won’t float any more  

Septembers bloodshed fits into  

The yearly average, Access denied  

To defy the hope that one may keep  

One million plus people need to feed  

Can 3500 truck loads sustain us?  

Denied pinenuts in our hummus  

Denied delivery at a hospital, check point  

Access denied, If I wake  

Before I die, let my soul  

Be free to fly, to any land  

Where Freedom flies, Access Denied.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-01-27*
