# My heart beats in rythm with your soul

My heart bleeds in rythm with your soul  

I stare as you stroll through my love  

Daring me to be stronger  

In a different way  

My unusual motion will make you pay  

But its not about what you will do  

Its about how much im willing to inflict  

Untill I feel like the villian  

That loves to predict, but  

Unaware of why  

This conflict exists  

To feel villified  

For something born inside  

Subject to its punishing pain  

Somedays I wake to curse the night  

Concieved in green and loves to fight  

The creative brain his birthright  

A litmus test of society, right  

Just call me a charlatan  

And what the fuck is a tarlatan?  

This is sounding contrived  

I am only an undergraduate  

Quite inadequate  

But entirely passionate  

So my heart beats in rythm with your soul  

I sleep as you stroll through my dreams  

We are riding a cloud on a gulfstream  

Having learned to sway  

Now I am begging you to stay.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-01-18*
