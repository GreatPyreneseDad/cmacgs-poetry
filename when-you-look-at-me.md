# When you look at me

When I look at you  

I feel an intense internal presence.  

Like your soul is reaching me  

When I look at you  

I can barely breath  

But a smile grows across this face  

I am wearing my heart on my sleeve  

When you look at me  

Do you feel the depth of my being?  

Can you see through my blue eyes  

or dimpled cheeks?  

Can you feel me?  

When I look at you  

Your soul its beauty shining and free  

This feeling I am feeling ecstasy  

When you look at me  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-08-04*
