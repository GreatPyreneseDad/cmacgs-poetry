# Earthquakes end in ache

I allow emotional earthquakes  

Sending pressure waves, social vibrations  

Disrupting academic concentration  

What noise a breaking heart makes.  

If you believe you have what it takes  

To love the thing that brings great irritation.  

Let us then break the bonds of a hateful nation  

Will we get married, make love and eat cake.  

Or will we fight, bicker and hate  

I know I can use my imagination  

To avoid dragging us into emotional complication  

With every logical string I state  

I begin to realize I am much too late.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-02-13*
