# The last words

I dreamt about you last night  

We loved each other and the feelings hadn’t left our hearts  

I would feel you as if it was real  

When I woke my heart was pounding  

And the distant memory of our love tugging at my heart strings  

I told you I love you  

And I always wanted that to be the last thing I said to you.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2020-05-16*
