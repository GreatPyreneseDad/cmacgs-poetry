# Breathing

Breathing in the air  

Feeling the deep presence  

Breathing out the air  

Feeling the deep effect  

Breathing in the air  

Seeing all that is  

Breathing out the air  

Knowing all that is not  

Breathing in the air  

Hearing the song of life’s rhythm  

Breathing out the air  

Singing along.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-11-18*
