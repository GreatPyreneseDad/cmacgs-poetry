# Evening after work

Blue ottoman and Venison dinners  

Not my kill  

Whole foods hunting  

I do not enjoy clothes  

To hot to handle  

Curly haired cuteness  

A white faced pooch  

and all the things  

The good things  

Possibly the best things  

Your touch is like  

Sour candy for the skin  

Evenings after work  

And long rides on the weekends  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-08-17*
