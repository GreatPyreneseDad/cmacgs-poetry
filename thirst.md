# Thirst

lost meaning  

never hurts to defame what you can’t touch  

is art to be possessed  

are artist “ART” themselves  

property  

forgotten answers  

always feels good to dance in the fame  

does it look nice on your wall  

next to you in a picture  

vainglorious masters  

playing a game  

everlasting torment and a sorrowful heart  

is there any place to rest  

a present moment longer than eternity  

a long deep breathe  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-08-30*
