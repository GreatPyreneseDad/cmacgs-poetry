# New people

I met you  

You in your world  

You wrapped up in your experiences  

You found a treasure but you will never know  

Until I put my hands around your fragile existence  

Your bubble of comfort  

And I crush it  

Welcome to my world  

You brand new being  

Have a taste of the greater world  

Have a taste of the edge  

I am dreaming of the one  

Who will find my boundaries  

The one who will cut me deep  

Among the redwoods and jade laden shores  

Among the the rivers edge  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-06-28*
