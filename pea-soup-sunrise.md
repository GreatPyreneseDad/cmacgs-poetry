# Pea Soup Sunrise

Don’t look me in the eyes  

Tell me you love me  

Just pull a thorn from my side  

And the walls of my tire  

Collapsing in on my adventure  

Love is like a bubble  

Collapsing on any breeze of air  

Can you see the lights  

Cavitation and emaciated feelings  

Grey skies for the weekend  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-04-30*
