# Night Ride

Meet me at the bridge  

above the tracks  

Yes, the one at the amtrack  

We can ride the trails  

all the way  

Sounds of amphibians  

Sounds of love  

When i peddle next to you  

I feel like you are wrapped  

Tighly in my arms  

I still feel that way even after  

I rode the same path all the way back to the big green house  

Where i sat and remebered  

Every moment of infinity  

I have been blessed with
