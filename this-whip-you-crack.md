# This Whip You Crack

This whip you crack  

Less money more work  

And no promise that  

I can pay the bill tomorrow  

Or the day after that  

You want the best of me  

But you can’t handle that i am  

More than a worker, or a hired hand.  

You tell me I did a good job  

Then threats to fire day after that  

I told you I was bipolar but  

I’m starting to find that  

It is you who is bipolar and completely whacked  

So take your three hour shift  

And shove it up your ass.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-11-01*
