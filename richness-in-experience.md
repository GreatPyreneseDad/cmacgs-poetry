# Richness In Experience

Up and down like the terrain  

Of Texas hill country  

One moment in time  

Do not deny yourself  

The richness of experience  

The more you talk  

The less you hear  

Do not deny yourself  

The richness of experience  

Of going far away from home  

Find that thing  

Hold on  

Find that thing  

Let go  

It is the same thing  

The begining starts with an end  

Do not deny yourself  

The richness of experience  

Up and down like the hills  

Of the Pacific coast  

One moment in time  

Has always been and  

Will always be  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-02-02*
