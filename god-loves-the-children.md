# God loves the children

Baby latino boy  

Kind brown eyes staring  

Smiling  

I can see why God loves the children  

His mother, baby girl on chest harness  

Concerned, scanning, vigilent  

Her love is his world  

I can see why God loves the children  

He mouths new words  

Stares upward in amusent  

Rubbing his head on her side, her hand  

Can you see why God loves the children?  

I can barely make eye contact with him  

Does he know he is subject of my poem?  

Beats bumping in my ears, 5 min out  

I can see why God loves the children.  

His camoflauge fleece  

Does he even know about war  

I pray he dreams of peace  

So he will know that God loves the children.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-01-24*
