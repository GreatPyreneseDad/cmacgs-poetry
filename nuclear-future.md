# Nuclear Future

So many mountains  

This desert says  

My sand is but dust from a windy day  

Years of it hide my trees  

Hilltops of history  

That floating note then melody  

The most beautiful violin  

ancient mahogany  

Smells of eucalyptus  

Views off cliffs by the sea  

All of society betrays us  

The lies and the greed  

Like desert sands upon us  

Drowning all the trees  

Hilltops rise above us  

The officials now royalty  

the most beautiful child from sin  

Nuclear proclivity  

Smells of Ozone  

Views of a dead economy  

All of society betrays us  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-12-14*
