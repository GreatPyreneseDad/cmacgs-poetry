# Ice Melt

i felt the ice melt  
it wasn't particularly warm  
and the white faded to brown and green  
and my soul started to shiver  

and the wet air felt colder  
than when the land had a blanket of white  
and the air was dry  

ice slicks now puddles  
my toes still dry in winter boots  
that will never dry  
in this sweltering lake land  

i've never considered  
that every effort i would take in life  
would be reaching for a time  
i no longer exist in  

and i question myself constantly  

then a bud  
a flower  
and the seasons fade into  
traffic jams and bbqs  
boat traffic and bikinis  

and i sit inside and wonder  
if the destination was always  
the moments between each step  

---

*Christopher MacGregor*
