# Josiah’s

Motorbikes over mountains  

These arms around my waist  

Waves wash over head  

Paddle until you cannot feel your arms.  

Find that perfect angle and ride the wave all the way into shore  

My heart never feels better than when it is bathed in salt water  

I watched the film over and over  

Each time it brings me back  

To the first part of our day  

Before the sunset on our love  

Until I found a way to ruin it all  

By being me  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-02-18*
