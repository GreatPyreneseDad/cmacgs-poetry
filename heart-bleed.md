# Heart bleed

When my heart beats, it is for you  

You had it from the first time I met you  

The flowers, parking lot , make out session  

The heavy beating of hearts in sync  

I made you strawberry pie  

You made me the apple of your eye  

I love you I truely do  

My betrayal between my ears  

I dream of the end of this year  

I pray to the lord, forgiveness  

That you may let me love you  

That I will even when you don’t  

I feel like a failure, the worst kind  

That nothing will ever matter as much  

As me walking away from you  

I wasn’t lieing when I told you  

When I said to you that summer  

That my heart will never heal  

From losing the light in my dark world  

Its been dark for too long  

I cannot see myself anymore  

A shadow on the wall at midnight  

I cannot breath without it  

I do not float anymore  

More alive in that year than the sum of my life  

I close my eyes and see you, my wife  

I open them and see me  

Heart bitter taste, in strife  

Can I come crawling back  

Will you accept me  

Or send me to pack  

Set my heart free  

Lara, my sweet Lara bee  

I open my heart to God so that thee  

Can love me the way Jesus loves me  

So my heart will be set free  

When I close my eyes and dream  

I see the white church in every scene  

The one I know I will build, because God showed me  

The reason for which I am still to breath.  

If it takes more than honesty you better believe  

If it takes a million years I will still bleed  

My love for his love, wholeheartedly  

So you may want to hold my hand through eternity.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-02-02*
