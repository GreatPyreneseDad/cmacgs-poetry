# Forests and fast dogs

Single track hiking path  

Four legged frenzy  

Canopies are comforting  

Climb?  

Its harder coming down  

Climb?  

Prayer flags on the power tree  

Bonny Doon redwood  

Leaf litter mixed moldy blankets  

Dry dusty duff and a million dollar view  

In every direction  

Try saying this Outloud  

“I spent my whole life looking up…  

When everything I wanted was underneath my feet.”  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-07-28*
