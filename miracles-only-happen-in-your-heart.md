# Miracles only happen in your heart

If I was a rock and you were the ocean  

Would you rub me smooth  

Or smash me into sand?  

Would you be warm or cold?  

Moss covered bird perch covered in life  

A hundred thousands years  

Together  

Another passing day  

Another moment  

A wave  

A beach  

A cool summer day  

The smell of the ocean  

The warmth of your hand  

The green in your eyes  

The love of your life  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-08-16*
