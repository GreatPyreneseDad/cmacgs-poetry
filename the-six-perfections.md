# The Six Perfections

When I dwell on the six perfections,  

A mind is awakened, generosity  

and other virtues  guide my heart  

with slack-less line.  



My mind twists and writhes, through eternal time.  

Awaken, Awaken concentration. To place effort,  

before desire, wisdom burns away ignorance, like flesh burning  

on the funeral pyre.  



Let me have patience while broken hearts scream “liar”.  

Ethics for the disciplined mind, requires,  

a strong heart, awaken, awaken,  

the mind, to find, you; six perfections.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-08-12*
