# Deluge

All this pressure and  
the walls collapsing  
All this sediment stirred up  
This dirty water rages forth  
From my lips  
To tare at the foundation  
of your confidence  
To eat away the rotting core  
Of this toxicity  
This constipation of the heart  
To break free from this bullshit  
And I can breathe  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2021-12-03*
