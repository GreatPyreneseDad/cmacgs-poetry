# Never Never Same Same

I dreamed about you last night  

Jean shorts and a smile  

Short hair and the warm glow of summer  

In my dream  

We embraced and you faded away  

Ive been searching 30 years for my love  

The only place I have ever found it  

Is in my own heart  

That’s the only place for each of us  

Where love truely exists  

A smile a kiss  

A midnight stroll along the Clark Fork  

Waking up to you asking you for a drag  

Off your Tobacco Marajuana ciggerete  

Instead you hand me  

The spliff you rolled for me  

White clouds of smoke  

pour from my lungs  

and I will never be the same again
