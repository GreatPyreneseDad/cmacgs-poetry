# Sand Dunes

the forest grows where roads cannot be maintained  

the bumps and dips of pavement  

send shock waves through travelers  

while the river is forced this way and that  

as it tries to unite with its lover the ocean  

the sun and the sky are like dancers  

among the heavens their movements  

stir the embrace of the wind  

celestial pirouettes form the smooth contours  

unimaginable lines waiting to be disturbed  

the jealousy of the tides  

the forest grows and the sea remains  

below the heavens and through the reign of time  

sand dunes covered in blankets of moss  

disguise the ever changing moods of the earth mother  

as she spins along the long beautiful path  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-06-22*
