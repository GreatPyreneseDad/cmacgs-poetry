# Learn To Dive

Something happened in me  

When you sent that message  

My heart has never felt so good  

There is not a word I know  

In any language that can describe  

The happiness I am feeling  

“I love you too”  

For as long as the sun rises  

To warm our hearts  

I will be a place for your love  

You never needed to say it  

It pours from  

Your beautiful brown eyes  

It resonates from your voice  

I feel it in every moment  

And every rise of my chest  

In the rythm  

of the beating of your heart  

I found a deep love  

Now I must learn to dive.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-26*
