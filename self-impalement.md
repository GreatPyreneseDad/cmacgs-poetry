# Self-impalement

Have you fallen on your own blade?  

You call it blood letting  

But it is not the same  

the time has come you’re not ready  

As you chase the gold and fame.  

If you were ugly they’d call it cutting  

You have only you to blame.  

Come to god or go find Jesus  

When you find your neck to blade  

You call it capital punishment  

State murder the name of the game  

The time has come you’re not ready  

As you chase the gold and fame  

All that youth and beauty fading  

You have only you to blame.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-07-24*
