# Wine Time Machine

Semonay on a breezy day  

Chardonnay in her pocket  

As we walk we wined and wind  

Through the halls of Chapultepec  

Back in time  

To a time before  

A time when honor was worth  

Just a little more  

Sunrise on the sleepy valley  

Back to work or back to play  

As we sleep we wined and wind  

Through the streets of Frisco bay.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-09-10*
