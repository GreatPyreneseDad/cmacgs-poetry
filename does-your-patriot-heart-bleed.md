# Does your patriot heart bleed?

Fog haze table gaze through glass  

Colors tell past to many dimension  

too fast.  

Gloria tells me pray to god.  

Mother mother in prison  

In another  

State, Utah Mormon fate.  

They work day to day to convert  

dead relatives, to spare themselves perceived hate.  

Irate is the mood inside the great salt lake.  

Lake to become rivers flowing into the sea, I cannot believe in another police state.  

Pushed down, beaten, the more you have the more they take.  

To late, to mother fucking late.  

Break… they break… they’ve…  

Made another race of slaves.  

How many hours have you baked for that pay?  

Do you have more dough at the end of the day?  

Fuck no! Is the consensus, but wait…  

Real fast the reel spins past the remembrance of the late history lesson, from my dreams, where it all comes back and relates.  

Some one please stab me in the patriots heart before I Wake.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-06-07*
