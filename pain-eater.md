# Pain eater

Let me tell you of my trouble  

Let me shine my light of wisdom  

From my pain  

Deep into a soul that is not mine  

Matter not your pain but mine  

Matter not a year or a moment  

An experience felt deep  

A lovely place to rest  

And under this next bridge  

A pain eater  

Open up and swallow  

because all Pain leads  

To a place of growth  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-06-22*
