# Stop Pretending

Nationalistic values now disgust me  

Feeling as if I have been duped my whole life.  

America the pitiful, the coward, the hypocrite  

The puppet of foreign interests  

A genocidal manifest destiny  

And nobody cares.  

A genocidal eugenics program for mentally ill,  

And nobody cares.  

We can watch all the movies in the world  

It doesn’t bring justice only apathy  

Towards future killing, maiming, and hate  

Only love begets love  

It exists in all of us  

Stop pretending  

You create your own reality  

Stop pretending  

The truth is universal  

Stop pretending  

The path is right before your eyes  

Wake up  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2014-07-31*
