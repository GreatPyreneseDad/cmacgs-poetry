# Shame on me

Go ahead  

Drag my name through the dirt  

But I am not my name  

I am not what you claim  

Only the truth teller Time  

Knows who i am  

Wind  

Or water  

Time and growth  

Truth is only heard  

Once those who live on lies  

Have starved to death  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-06-22*
