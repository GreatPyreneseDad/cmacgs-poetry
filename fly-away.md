# Fly Away

My heart has broken  

A thousand times  

I thought I would be used to it  

But everytime is like the first  

I can feel it on every beat  

The taste of your lips  

And all the things you said to me  

Why would you say those things?  

I am going to fly away  

To a place where they speak truth  

Where the first thing is  

I love you  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-02-01*
