# Empowerment

She said I Cannot  

Remember feeling so free  

although you have me dressed in chains  

I can feel every drop of blood  

Course through my viens  

Your Hand Squeezes my throat  

Yet it’s never been easier to breathe  

She said I feel a thousand feet tall  

When you force me to my knees  

It is never more pleasurable for me  

When i must beg and plead  

For the way of lovers is opposite  

In the wise words of Rumi  

Like an unbroken stallion  

Fun to ride but hard to break  

She said the less you give  

The more I crave  

Do not make love to me  

I want to feel depraved  

She said I want to call you Master  

But I will never be your Slave.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-06-15*
