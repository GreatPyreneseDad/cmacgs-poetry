# Through Crystal Irises

I will dodge the rage and terror  

Or deflect it with my soul  

I was meant for such harsh weather  

Take refuge in the wake or fall  

Were my soul see’s storm break  

If you let go just keep afloat  

Times cycle repeats for  if you need another tow  

Simply,  raise your light to next falls flow  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-02-19*
