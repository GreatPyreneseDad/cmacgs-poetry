# Anachronous

Flightless birds  

Another tall tale  

From the halls of history  

Like a dinosaur you mean  

No not a lizard  

Just an Emu  

Flightless birds  

A shameful statement  

Guilty of shame  

Nothing more  

Head in the sand  

One leg in the mud  

No not a lizard  

I think you mean a dinosaur  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2017-09-18*
