# Blood Stained Flags of Our Fathers

This patriots soul inside us  

Refuses to abide by our sense of fear  

All these years all these years  

Of secrets, lies, theft of dreams  

Our hearts scream out ‘justice”  

Only hearing the reply by machine  

All these years all these years  

Blood stained flags of our fathers  

Burning in the gentleman’s ears  

Must we follow until we die?  

Or choose to live in secret.  

What is left to be sacred?  

To be revered.  

To cherish what is love  

To desire having no desire  

To burn the leash upon the fire  

Blossom and bloom freedoms flowers  

love awakening what is true and right  

Grow the vines to hold the path  

throw the pigs onto their backs.  

This patriots soul inside us  

Refuses to abide by our sense of fear  

All these years all these years  

Blood stained flags of our fathers  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-12-07*
