# Manifesting Mandates

If you grab at the wind  

The others will call you “fool”  

While they grab at the tide  

A position in the sky  

Who sees the moon each night  

A position on the ground  

Who recieves sun  

and those who recieves draught  

bath in the sun day and night  

Who sleeps and who turns  

Is there any where to run  

When you need vacinations  

Nations  

Who begs and who gives  

Who pretends and what is real to you  

These vague notions beg for your attention  

Abracadabra  

Who says what it is  

You  

knowing you  

You Manifesting mandates  

Awakened Now

*Christopher MacGregor — 2017-01-03*
