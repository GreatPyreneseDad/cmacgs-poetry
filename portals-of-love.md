# Portals of Love

Walking along this water flowing  

No one else knows  

Vacant forest and a dead tree  

Spans the Creek  

Where I will ask you to marry me  

The confluence  

The solstice  

Water signs along ruff edges  

And you told the pregnant woman  

Who told the whole class  

Then it wasn’t just ours any more  

They all wanted a piece  

So we gave it all away  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-12-19*
