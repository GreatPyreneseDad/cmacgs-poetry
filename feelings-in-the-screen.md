# Feelings In The Screen

Did you see the waves running through the wayside?  

Or are you staring at your feet?  

Can we find ourselves in the ether?  

Songs about murder and drugs  

Sing along to the breezeblocks  

not knowing its sinister feel  

just catchy tunes and a hook  

Songs about heartbreak and selfish thoughts  

Sing along on the radio  

Four chords and a wet tampon  

just blood flavored ketchup  

Songs about feminized men and feelings on the screen  

Did you see her hairs sticking out of the nylons?  

Sharing your bed with an adult child seem fun?  

Did you get lost in the dead man’s philosophy?  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-08-24*
