# Sour Fruit

I was chasing love  

Now i’m chasing my tail  

Broken like a stone  

Upon this harsh coastline  

Where souls break on  

each others ruff and sharp edges  

My heart feels bruised  

Like a bag of fruit  

Thrown down flights of stairs  

All the sweetness leaking out  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-02-01*
