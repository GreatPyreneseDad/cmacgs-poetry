# Pushing for Peace

Grinding my gears down this path  

Small trees Great Lakes  

I often wonder why I don’t think  

Deeply while turning the wheels  

One push at a time  

Each mile less thought  

Each mile more peace  

Until I’m glowing  

My heart is full  

No matter where I am  

Here I am not lost  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-07-12*
