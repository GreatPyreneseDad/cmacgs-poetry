# Excuse me miss

Excuse me miss  

Can I be obtrusive for a moment  

Just look down these lines  

On this face of mine  

Word just words pumping hormonal  

Release yourself from inner worry  

Release yourself from regret  

Find the moment where everything  

Fades from view, the past, the future  

Now taboo.  

Focus on feeling , as it stimulates  

your spines nervous net.  

Like butterfly’s in your stomach like that feeling of falling in a dream  

Release yourself from inner fret.  

The words are here to catch you  

Or to remind you it’s not over yet  

But words are not expression  

You must be alive to produce that.  

Release yourself from doubt  

Pardon me miss, the world is amiss  

So you won’t mind if I readily dismiss  

This false reality feeding my dreams  

I am like eternal energy  

Release yourself from depravity.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-10-03*
