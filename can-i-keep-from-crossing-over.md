# Can I keep from crossing over?

Can I keep from crossing over?  

How can my heart keep beating  

After every time I break yours  

I obliterate mine  

Can I keep from crossing over?  

If I can do it in pairs  

Without you noticing  

Then I will be on the same side when you do  

Can I keep from crossing over?  

Rolling waves do yell  

Lifting my head above then below the horizon  

Silhouette of my soul shining in the sun  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-02-01*
