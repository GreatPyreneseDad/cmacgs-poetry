# Thalassophile

Wild windy whipped  

Water  

Calm blue sea  

Vanishing reef  

Up and down  

Up and down  

The roll and the whitecap  

Another calm glistening night  

A golden lunar road to nowhere  

Salty lips  

Another sunset on westcliff  

Another deep breathe  

And the smell you can’t forget  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2017-02-28*
