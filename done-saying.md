# done saying

I have nothing to say  

Nothing feels good  

just silence don’t speak  

Nothing but our voices  

Can ruin the peace  

Don’t say just be  

You can know everything  

If you look into my eyes  

Every vertical peak  

A mountain A soul  

Winter is a blanket  

Rain sounds on helmets  

A horizontal sunbeam  

Rainbows and blood  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-12-27*
