# Forest and Crumbling Coastlines

I cannot swallow this  

Eating is difficult  

When the wind is blowing  

Fast and Sharp  

Ragged Point had no effect  

Tired legs do not exhaust the heart  

Nor do they cure the mind  

If i could break myself  

A thousand times upon  

This lovers shore  

Believe I already have  

I have been here before  

A thousand lifetimes  

End to end  

Beginning to beginning  

has it ever been different?  

Forest and crumbling coastlines  

One the solution to the other  

Waves of love  

Jade hearts  

Wet with the tears of the earth  

Let there be an end  

Or let it begin  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-23*
