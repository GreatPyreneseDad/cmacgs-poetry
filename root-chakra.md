# Root Chakra

When I cross from the metaphysical  
The misty morning grasps at my skin.  
I feel uprooted, although  
This is how it has always been.  

Like a freight train the emotions slam  
Center mass, like a well place blow to the ego.  

I often outrun feelings in my dreams  
Yet they always catch up in my waking sleep.  

To find more space inside for this universe  
om mani padme hum, om mani padme hum.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2014-07-30*
