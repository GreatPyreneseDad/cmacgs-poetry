# Jade Truce

When the ocean met the heart of the earth.  

They formed a truce.  

For they knew that they would eventually destroy each other.  

The heart being so hot , so grounded by its own enormous density said water, we could fight, at our borders. But what would that do for us.  

I would be cooled and you would be warmed. But neither of us would be us anymore.  

I would cease to move and you would float away.  

The ocean listened to the heart and believed this to be true. But the ocean loved the land dearly but she also knew she had an awful effect on it.  

When the ocean met the land the land crumbled away becoming sand on the oceans floor.  

And when the ocean grabbed for its love the sand would wisp away out of sight.  

The ocean said I will not fight you heart of the earth, but I ask of you one gift, on the eves of our great battles of past , I ask that you give to me the hardest rocks with hearts of jade and gold, so that I cannot wash them away from my grasp.  

The heart of the earth agreed and it set to making seven special places on the planet where the land meets with the ocean. Where it created a stone so hard even the ocean couldn’t turn it to sand, only round it smooth like the surface of the water on a calm day.  

And so the Ocean and the heart of the earth have lived in harmony, and every so often they meet again where the jade stones and gold can be found on the ever changing coastlines of earth.
