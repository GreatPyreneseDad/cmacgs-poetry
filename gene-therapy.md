# Gene therapy

Genetics test review  

How narrow its view  

While I am dreaming grandiosity  

Dr. refers to microscopy.  

Even linked genes cannot keep  

From crossing over  

Our will is frozen  

Into the permafrost from our chosen.  

How can we begin to believe  

That great minds ideas only decieve  

What perfect system leaves no room to breath?  

Like in life, one must struggle to be seen.  

If the concept of beauty is just a dream  

Then I will still fail to not believe  

That loves light guides us, moves our feet  

Confine my body, my soul is free.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-02-08*
