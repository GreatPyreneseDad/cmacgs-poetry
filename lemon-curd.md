# Lemon Curd

Sunny red deck where I lay  

Husky holes in the sand  

Lemon juice, egg yokes  

Suger, butter  

Hemlock trees and toad stools  

Forests paths where you may find  

A smile among the moss  

Sunny red deck  

Bicycles everywhere  

Family dinners with strangers  

New friends  

Another time perhaps, another year  

Stir it until it thickens to ribbons  

Another time perhaps  

Another life  

Ribbons  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-10-25*
