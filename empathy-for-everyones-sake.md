# Empathy for Everyones Sake

When you look up through the leaves  

Do you ever notice how they glow?  

When you walk by a plant  

Do you crush up a part of it to see how it smells?  

When you look into an animals eyes  

Have you noticed that it is thinking?  

Do you ever feel  

What others may be feeling?  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-07-15*
