# Because I love you

I spent more than a year  

Sitting here working myself with memories  

Every morning trying to pry myself open  

So I could feel the reality of what was  

The mind heals faster than the heart  

But we can’t really feel the scars  

We can only feel the wounds  

Healing is more than not feeling pain  

Fresh wounds for breakfast  

Painting in the afternoon  

Because I love you  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-06-06*
