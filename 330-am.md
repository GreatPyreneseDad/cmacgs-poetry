# 330 Am

Is it just the empty heart  

This warmth felt by any  

traveler  

or person in need of  

warmth  

Is it just an empty heart  

This need to connect  

To the space inside  

each other  

Would it be better  

If I knew nothing about you  

Its 330 am  

I can feel it happening again  

Just my empty heart  

beating  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-02-28*
