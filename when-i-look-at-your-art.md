# When I look at your art.

When I look at your art.  

The pulse increases in my heart.  

I struggle for breathe or even thoughts.  

Like lightning it strikes me, your soul, my god.  

It is beautiful, every waking piece.  

Like thunder it’s frightening, your beauty, my god.  

I struggle to hold concentration or to think.  

The pulse increases in my heart.  

When I look at your art.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-07-20*
