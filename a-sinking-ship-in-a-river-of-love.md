# a sinking ship in a river of love

sometimes perception is not reality  

what you think you feel may just be biological formality  

the places from which our love arises  

maybe as distant as the moon  

But we have all witnessed the tides  

each shore line as different as each owns mind  

To look at the sun in bright day light burns ones eyes  

But haven’t we all felt the warmth in a slow sunrise after a cold night outside?  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-06-05*
