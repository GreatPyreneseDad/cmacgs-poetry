# Cherry Trees 

Forests surround me I am home  

More free than a skipped rock over  

The Clark-Fork river  

My edges rolled smooth by the journey  

Forests like my blood fill me with air  

Like a bright red cherry juice  

Its lifeforce pulses through my veins  

Cherry Trees and long journeys home  

Single track and Grizzley bears  

I am running in my dreams  

From the monsters of love  

A tall tree stands over me  

Shadows welcoming rest  

A fallen giant  

Red skin and Fungus  

Skipping stones on the Pacific  

A small bird sings me love songs  

As my feet dance in circles  

Cherry Trees on puddles of ancient oceans  

Flooded valleys ripple forth  

Tornadoes in my dreams  

Nothing left of my heart but this  

Small Jade pebble  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-09-08*
