# Chopping Block

I am dying of a broken heart  

It didn’t happen easily  

My heart on top the chopping block  

Swinging my axe repeatedly.  

Now my heart is but kindling  

As I am dying of a broken heart  

I can smell the love burning  

But it was me who doused it in gasoline.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-07-02*
