# Tell me

Don’t tell me it’s drama  

Because the love i feel  

Is louder than the ocean  

On the back of a winter storm  

Don’t tell me I don’t love you  

That I barely know you  

Because what was a week for you  

Lasted an eternity for me  

Every memory of you for the past  

Thousand years beats upon my soul  

Don’t tell me anything  

You thinking of me is enough  

When we die and are reborn  

We will find each other once more  

To remind the other of that time we danced in the kitchen and ate the bounty of the forest  

When we woke from our dreams  

all that was left was you  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-24*
