# The trouble in falling asleep

Somedays I’m filled with sadness  
Somedays it’s all I eat.  
Gut wrenching sensations.  
I have trouble falling asleep.  

Pain of hunger preferred to the pain of mind.  
Inside is a raging battle for my soul and for my life.  
Gut wrenching sensations.  
I am more than timelines.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-05-02*
