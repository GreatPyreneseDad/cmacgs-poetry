# My Sinking Shadow

The days are shorter  

One rotation at a time  

I climb this mountain of my heart  

One place or another  

I find myself in the salted tears  

Running down my forehead  

My body cries happy and free  

Sweat that liquid of effort  

Love that emotion of heartache  

That place where we all end up  

Embracing this pain I feel  

Whole again  

It is no longer about anyone else  

I turn my back on feelings I once  

Held close to my heart  

But i cannot forget  

Nor regret where I have been  

If I could paint a picture  

Of my love for you  

It would be the deepest shade  

Shadows and Jade  

I come from ashes and mud  

My sinking shadow becomes  

This very earth  

Where ever you step  

I will feel the beating of your heart.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-16*
