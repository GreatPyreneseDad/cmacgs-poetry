# I am …

I am lost  

I am broken  

I am a sinner  

I am loved  

Unconditionally  

I am forgiven  

There is a deep bottomless hole in all of us  

God fills that hole completely or allows you to live empty.  

Everything good can disappear into that hole or everything good can come out of it.  

I am lost but I have been found  

I am a sinner but I have been forgiven  

I am loved and I feel it  

Unconditionally  

I am forgiven but I haven’t forgotten  

This hole inside me is a mountain  

Where god lives.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-12-29*
