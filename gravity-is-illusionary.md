# Gravity is illusionary.

There in the horizon far from darkness  
He see’s the sky fall  
Frozen in his mind this image set in eternal time  
winding past the edge of the universe.  

We find each other.  
That place in our hearts where the love ignites  
A feeling that one can not affix a price  
winding past the edge of the universe.  

We stop to take in the view.  
There is no air to breathe, or things to see  
We live on love, that sustaining principle of eternity  
winding past the edge of the universe.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-09-24*
