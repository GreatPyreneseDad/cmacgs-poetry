# I’m sorry.

Popping the bubble  

Your face is more than skin  

Muscles of your face  

Facial expressions  

To look as if you feel  

Expressionless angel in the clouds  

Can you feel me now?  

The arm stretching long  

Wasteful inexcusably melody  

Of truth or of lies  

Why do we have to hide?  

From what we feel.  

Give us a new deal, a new day.  

Owner of the club  

Trust fund baby on display  

Why do we have to say ?  

I’m sorry.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-08-03*
