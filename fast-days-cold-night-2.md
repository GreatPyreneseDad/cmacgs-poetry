# Fast Days Cold Night

I got a really thin sleeping bag  

So I could shiver along side you  

When we are sleeping  

In our tents alone  

I had to put my dogs blanket  

Around my feet inside the bag  

The funny thing is  

I slept better and dreamed more  

Than anytime I felt warm  

I got a bike and i loaded it down  

With hundreds of pounds of gear  

And a dog that you admire  

So I could understand what you  

Feel when you are moving along  

My birthplace and home  

Now that i’m in the midst of this  

Long road of adventure  

I have found  

A deep ocean is your soul  

Fast days and cold nights  

Under oak trees and redwoods  

For 30 years I have never been somewhere more beautiful than these moments rediscovering  

The love I hold in my heart  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-29*
