# Long Rides

Long rides  

Like a long sigh of fresh air  

Cold crisp satisfaction  

Long rides leave room  

For introspection  

A checklist inspection  

Of your body and mind  

And every kilometer that passes  

Is never enough to satisfy  

That longing for more  

The best cure for a heavy heart  

Is a tired body  

Long rides sharpen my love  

For this world and its beauty  

Tired legs are an invitation  

For more  

It may be better to have exhausted ones heart through  

the legs than through the mouth  

Long rides even in days when the starting point is the destination.  

Long rides so my heart may blossom into a seed of love  

to grow its roots into the hearts of all I pass.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-13*
