# Sultry Street Dancer

sultry street dancer  

he walks feet firing faster  

his goal is her deepest fantasy  

his feet become like fire,  

his body is thunder.  

She can feel the vibrations  

before she can feel the heat  

his feet firing faster,  

his goal her lingering ecstasy  

his hands are bright light,  

his mind the master  

she, fallen beauty awaiting his violence  

her legs smooth, twisted together  

like the vines from a forbidden tree,  

fruit tempting  

but he is driven by something greater, temptations forgotten  

he is hers, and she knows it,  

thunder growing closer  

her body craves it, her fear forbodes  

lingering thoughts of that time before,  

to get back there climbing that hillside of pleasure, till the steep drop  

he catches her with strong arms, sensing vulnrability  

his hips firing faster, lips intertwined like vines her body uncontrollable,  

completely in his control she twists and writhes and rides and rides  

until her soul is thunder,  

her legs like the wind  

a strong gust spins them into the air  

a thunderous bolt of lightning they disappear.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-04-25*
