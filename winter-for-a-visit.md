# Winter For a Visit

Winter came and went  

Leaving it’s sadness and cold touch  

On my heart  

Spring couldn’t warm me up  

So far from where I started  

A place of warmth and love  

A sad story in a broken pane of glass  

A reflection on a rippled pond of sorrow  

I never found that place I left  

The road ahead is paved in hard moments  

Scraped knees or broken hearts  

the only prize  

Open eyes can’t help but see  

The best times have gone  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-04-02*
