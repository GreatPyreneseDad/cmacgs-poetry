# A poem for my Mother

Oh to suffer, I rage.  

All your lies set center stage.  

Upstaged by the way you say “truth”.  

Like an echo with no source.  

The sounds don’t place you.  

Inside your mind, you see false reality.  

Your eyes are blind.  

Vision blurred by all the ways you lie.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-04-27*
