# Jade Cove

It has never rained on Jade Cove  

In my presence  

I don’t believe it ever will  

Just sunlight and ocean spray  

The large waves keep tourists  

Off my prime hunting grounds  

Spinning green jewels  

Like shooting stars  

Launched by waves  

To the beach under my feet  

Keen ears listen  

As eyes search the ground  

A rumble and roar  

Quick feet take bounty and hunter  

To saftey of dry rocks  

Climbing cliffside trails  

To a camp made of memories  

It has never rained on me  

At Jade Cove  

And I don’t believe it ever will  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-12-27*
