# A million possibilities

You either are or you are not, she says  

The long drive home builds armor where there were tears  

Her fears materialized, but they do not stick.  

Hanging around in this cloud world  

Until the sun evaporates the sidewalk  

I have fallen back into loves medium  

My heart stops while gazing at your eyes,stop.  

Pop, goes the weasel, eisel  

Art in my love spilling forth  

Like a painted meadow at sunrise  

The dew reflecting a million possibilities.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-02-27*
