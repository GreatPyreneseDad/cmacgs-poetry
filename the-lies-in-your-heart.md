# The lies in your heart

The protector in my heart  

It is dying to start, sacraficing  

Slicing apart  

Humanity cannot restart  

Start, to realize  

That you played your harp  

To the tune of an abuser  

Addicted to the pain, do my words strike  

Moon in the sky pulling stars  

Your harpstring, vibrating  

Pulsating with your soul  

Resonate with the others or fall  

The abuser would have you be one string  

On a grand harp of dillussion  

His fist, your illusion, of love  

a perquisite function operating above  

Conscious pride, why do I have to hide  

From what I feel, while the abuser is free  

To spin his wheels, burning rubber  

Thick black smoke.  

Choke, choked, revoked  

The lies in your heart  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-03-10*
