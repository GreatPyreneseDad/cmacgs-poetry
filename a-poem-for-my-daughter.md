# A Poem for My Daughter

holding on tightly when there are only moving things,  
my definition of faith  
that's how i see a human soul dancing through time  
a stone cast far  
a lost soul  
life doesn't need you to find it  
you are not lost  
you are love  

the boulders don't get a vote  

/ sinking crumbling Pyrite  
/ river bottom blues  
/ rippling rapids through my heart  
/ so the core can feel what its like to be a grain held by love  
/ so god can breathe  
/ breathe soul into existence  
/ breathe  
/ the grains of your heart  

---

*Christopher MacGregor*
