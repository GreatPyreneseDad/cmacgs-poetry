# Sympathy

My mother used to say quit often  

“Paranoia will destroy ya”  

Now I realized that’s because she had so much to hide.  

All these little saying she used to rant about  

i will list a few  

Hot Diarrhea in a Dixie cup  

(This phrase used to describe men she would see around town usually after saying “he thinks hes cool shit but..”  

The worlds not black and white its grey  

(this one really gets me…I used to think the woman was colorblind, because the world is colorful to me)  

I remember very little of our conversations besides these few tidbits of recurring visions.  

She is still alive, but it doesn’t feel like it.  

Thief, Meth addict, Betrayer of family, Child abuser, Alcoholic, Delinquent Parent, Manipulator of the dead’s will, Unrepentant all describe my thoughts of her actions.  

She could do the right thing and come clean in letters, but she would rather play for sympathy.  

When it’s clear to see that we have gone far to long for Sympathy  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-06-16*
