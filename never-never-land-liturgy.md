# Never Never Land Liturgy

River run this summer fun  

On hills of golden sun  

Made my day in whitewater  

The river laughing  

Wet soggy dogs on logs  

Tracking across the slow hole  

Set angle and swim  

Poop box pantry open for brunch  

Sunlight slow burn slippery sunscreen or round rocks reticulated moss chunks  

like a bearded lady sunbathing  

waving weaves whiped on water  

where do we go from here?  

downstream downtime  

hanging hammocks  

honey bear black forest  

and a newly discovered  

never never land  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-07-13*
