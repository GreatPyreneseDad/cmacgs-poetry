# Why do you spy?

Why do you spy?  

Why is it the way you spend your day.  

Not referencing a real job,  

Just the stroke and key.  

But maybe that is your real job.  

Maybe that is what you do for pay.  

Not referencing a google search  

Who even uses aol now anyway?  

You used to have to listen,  

Now you can profile words at play  

Not referencing the patriot act  

I’m talking about you stalking me.  

Don’t we have laws against this?  

Have we not seen this coming from miles away, yet they keep on coming.  

Making more than millions a day.  

Why do you spy, oh big brother?  

I thought you had my back like Canada, Eh? I went to war.  

Listened to what you had to say.  

Now some of us have realized.  

More and more learn everyday.  

The blinds are open, oh big brother  

It’s all out there as you say.  

So if you have nothing to hide.  

Why are you hiding it all?  

Every bleeding American day  

My country’s heart is broken.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-06-18*
