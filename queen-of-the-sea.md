# Queen Of The Sea

The wind picked me up  

It was the queen of the sea  

Her body a wind swept dune  

Curving into the beach  

“Bom dia sol” she said  

The light is to be  

Her golden crown  

The wind her loving embrace  

A smile from her is like  

The most beautiful landscape  

At sunset while the sky is turning into a coral reef.  

Quenn of the sea  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-02-03*
