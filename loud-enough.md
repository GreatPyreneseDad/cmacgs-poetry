# Loud Enough?

I wanted to climb the rafters  

To scream from the rooftops  

“I Love You”  

So you could hear me over  

The sounds of life  

This road, the waves, your music  

Aircraft engines spinning  

This idea has me asking  

How far is too far?  

How long is too long?  

A sunkissed princess  

The queen of the sea  

Every single heart beat  

That propels me from  

Where I am to where you are  

How many rotations until  

I’m full enough of love?  

How many kilometers  

Must I climb  

For you to hear me  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-02-06*
