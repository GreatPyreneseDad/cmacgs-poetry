# Parsnips by The Ocean

Can we live near the ocean?  

Grow some roots  

Parsnips and diver scallops  

I wanted to know where love comes from  

So I can be there all the time  

Tagliatelle al dentè for the stranger  

On the bicycle loaded down  

Uphill to get here  

Downhill to leave  

Full bellies and full hearts  

Parsnips and scallops  

where love  

comes beauty  

comes life  

Comes living  

Comes home  

I wanted to know where love comes from  

and now I know  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2017-03-08*
