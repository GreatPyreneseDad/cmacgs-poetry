# Dead Spin Cycle

I have a great interest in dying  

In failing to breathe again  

In allowing my blood to pool  

Sacraficial lamb of ignorance  

Slit my throat to honor the god of lies  

Pierce my heart to honor the god of greed, hate and suffering.  

Cut off my head as tribute to evil.  

We lost along time ago  

We have now but to suffer in ineptitude  

Believing slavery is freedom  

That ignorant lies are truth  

That greed and flatery are love  

That wealth is security  

That wealth is power  

That its always been this way  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2014-04-09*
