# Unequipped

When you look at me I feel  

What you called a chapter in our lives  

Three dates feels like eternity  

I get lost in the moments  



No? you say you don’t feel it yet  

With my memory intact I begin to regret  

Not you, nor it, just how I am a lie to forget  

Forgiven in love yet not in the flesh  



My biggest mistake was my ignorance  

How could I not know I was not ready yet  

But I chased it anyway, now I feel sick  

In the back of my throat, a frog, it sits  



I cannot swallow I cannot forget  

Those striking brown eyes hair so thick  

No make up can hide her beauty is rich  

And I cannot deny the life I don’t live.  



So here I stand, like a tree in the wind  

My leaves senesced, my heart has quit  

Without your love I am unequipped  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-10-17*
