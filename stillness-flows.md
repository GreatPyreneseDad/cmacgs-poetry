# Stillness Flows

Staring forward into the wall  

Body melting away entering the mind  

The world inside is so vast  

The thoughts created this place  

But what is it  

A place where thoughts are born  

Or are they borrowed?  

This place of a thousand lifetimes  

A thousand rebuttals to a monotonous continuation of humaities suffering  

Deep in my mind I find eternity  

Seeing no value in the struggles of the ego  

Stillness flows into the vast spaces  

Unreachable by our bodies  

Our mind is the ship  

The sacred temple our bodies  

Our souls the passenger  

The constant in this journey  

Can you remember your past  

Before you had lived once more  

If you cannot perhaps take a walk  

Into your mind  

Staring forward at the wall  

And looking back though time  

Walking in the vast space available  

To a human mind  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2017-09-07*
