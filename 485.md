# If I had loved

Walking backwards in time  

To the street along the beach were we held hands at sunset. Hearing your voice again.  

I still can smell you on the wind  

Although you left me long ago  

It doesn’t feel like you are far  

I like to make believe that we are connected in some special way that cannot be explained.  

Like we are soul mates and if we are to never kiss again in these bodies it will be but a short seperation on this eternal cosmic journey.  

I still remember that period of laying in bed untill late into the day. I remember forcing cigerettes into my lungs to be closer to you so as to keep your smoky taste from inhibiting my need to touch my mouth to your lips.  

The way you have only love and compassion in your heart. The inner beauty that shines through and the pain you keep invisible from all but the closest to you. I often question if I can go on without your knowledge of how I feel. I don’t know why this is important to me. To have my feelings reciprocated. The question we all must awnser, is it enough to just live on the love I have in my own heart?

*Christopher MacGregor — 2014-02-24*
