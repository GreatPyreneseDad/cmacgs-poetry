# Black Dot

Have you ever felt like you once had wings?  

That presence of fresh atmosphere all around you.  

That feeling you get when you”re falling down.  

Thats what it feels like when they cut them off.  

I can still see your face in my dreams.  

Touched by the innocence in your expression.  

When i am touched  

I crumble into darkness  

Your hand unlike midas but destructive all the same.  

A glimpse of gold to then rot away.  

Everything falls down they like to say.  

I hope I was not your big mistake.  

As you quickly make your grand escape.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-07-21*
