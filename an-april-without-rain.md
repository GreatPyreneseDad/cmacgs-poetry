# An April Without Rain

In the fog and the rain  
A series of choices  
An ignorant child with little sense  
Describes me , unworthy, un-worldly  
Her light is shinning and when I peak  
Through electronic blinds  
I can feel the hole that has grown  
Inside this chest of broken promises  
The possibility of love  
May but only germinate upon fertile soul  
Where can one find water in this desert?  
My love  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2014-08-09*
