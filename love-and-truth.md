# Love and Truth

I felt like dying when I realized  

That most would rather hide their heart  

Than risk rejection or  falling apart  

I felt like dying when I realized  

Speaking your mind will get you shot  

Specifically when loves the subject  

Love of country love of you  

Fool for both fool for truth  

Finding nothing but paved streets  

Parking lot blues  

They say let something go if you love it  

So I’m letting go of love and truth  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2014-02-05*
