# 18 a day

Everyday that I wake  

My soul trembles as I quake  

At the thought that I lose  

18 brothers a day  

well that is the figure many state  

I have heard in the news  

I have read in the blogs  

That 18 a day join the fog  

Bring your tears and ill bring mine  

These men once full of love  

Crushed and rubbed dry  

Unable to teer forward only die  

The pain so strong inside  

First they tear up there family  

Full of.pains. ghosts in the mind  

They search but never find  

What it means to just live  

Just to love and live  

They feel unworthy of such reward  

Extinguishing one life often exits two  

They are all colors not just red white blue  

Damaged and needing to be renewed  

Waiting so long for what they deserve  

Love love love is kind  

Show these souls love
