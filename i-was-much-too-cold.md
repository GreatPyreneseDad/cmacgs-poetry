# I was much too cold

It’s never too late  

I’ve been told, I’ve seen hearts break  

I’ve seen piles of gold  

Forked over on the premise of love.  

But something’s cannot be bought  

Or sold.  

Heartbreak or something that was.  

Take all your fucking piles,  

Wealth makes my bones cold.  

Too late mr. Lover man  

Too late, driving corvettes, you fool.  

You could of done the right thing.  

I have ripped wholes through my soul.  

I am missing two parts of me.  

I told myself never again after the first time.  

I sold myself on that goal.  

Well failure is my grand prize.  

I am drowning in this hole.  

I can see up through the tears.  

See you standing arms full.  

No room to embrace me, the fanciful fool.  

So I drift, heart break, sharp knife  

Can I operate on my own heart  

The answer is no.  

Like giant thistles in my heart.  

I take drugs, we rolled.  

To that dead mau concert, sex.  

How could I of known?  

That memory is priceless in my mind.  

I just want you to know.  

For me it’s not to late. Ill wait you go.  

My children never made this world.  

I was much too cold.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-06-21*
