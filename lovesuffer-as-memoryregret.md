# Love:Suffer as Memory:Regret

I still feel the way I did that day  

Reacting still to a picture or the name  

When I realized a general trend  

When my former loves remove my pictures from their facebook page  

Like how the winner always writes the history so be it with love  

But it’s unprecidented to be the subject of willful forgetting  

I spend my days in these painful envelopes of space time  

Because to suffer is to love in my mind  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2014-03-26*
