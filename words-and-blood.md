# Words and Blood

I have tied knots in my tongue  

To keep it from slipping from my mouth  

Words betraying me as my heart bleeds out  

It could not be easier than i have made it  

To slit the throat of my confidence  

So my breath stops short of my mouth  

Now their are no words to betray me  

And I have bled out  

So my breath stops short of my mouth  

I have tied knots in our love  

To keep it from running through my hands  

Love is slippery when wet with blood  

With the blood on the outside  

My heart is free from burden  

And my mind is free from doubt  

I have tied knots in my tongue  

To keep it from slipping from my mouth  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-07-01*
