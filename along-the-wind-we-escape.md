# Along the Wind we Escape

I am trapped in this world  

By a majority of reductionists  

Grounded by pessimists  

Disenchanted by ignorance  

No Matilda it  

Is not what you think it is  

It really? What gives.  

Just love  

Fuck Ohio, and the B&O  

Monopoly money and greenbacks  

Fake and Cold  

Just love that is  

Complete and true  

Upheaval escapes you  

But I can only leave you  

I do not want to misconstrue  

Would it be cool if I lack.  

Keep you in my heart?  

like a million dollar heart attack  

Better gets better when you cannot betray  

Burn the tarp on the track 1,000 hearts break a day  

Victims of indifference societies dismay.
