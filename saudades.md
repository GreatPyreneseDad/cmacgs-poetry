# Saudades

A little of that longing taste in your heart  

Unexplainable non-translatable idea  

Starts with feeling  

Ends with a feeling  

A kiss  

A smell  

A familiar touch on your face  

That touches your soul  

If i could exist in that place  

An eternity just won’t do  

Saudades  

My love is like a river  

Fed by the beating of my heart  

And i’ve been washed out to sea  

For far to long.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-12-23*
