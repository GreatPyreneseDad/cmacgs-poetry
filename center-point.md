# Center Point

The limited perception of fate  

Gives rise to false identity  

When we believe we are more than  

Exactly what we are at the moment  

The beauty of our unknown impact  

Into each others fates, life and death  

Success and suffering  

All inches from one another  

The thoughts we hold become  

So much more than thoughts  

They become the inches  

The very fabric of our experience  

If the universe is infinite  

Then every single point is at the center           Start to think you are not within it  

You are the universe. It is within you.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2014-05-05*
