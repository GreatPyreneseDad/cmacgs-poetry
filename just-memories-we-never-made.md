# Just Memories We Never Made

All your pictures on the pretty screen  

I could look but only see curves of slate  

eyes like the sea  

darkness beneath beauty  

but what I’m after is more elusive  

harder to see  

it lies beneath the skin and stretches  

from the end of one universe  

to high above the redwood trees  

I want to dive into your deep dark ocean  

drink from your creeks and to sleep  

within the furthest reaches of the coldest cave  

within that place where all the light has faded  

And when I emerge your images will no longer be pictures  

but memories of the time when our souls danced among the stars  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-02-26*
