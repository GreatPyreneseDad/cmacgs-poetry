# Two stanza’s of ego

Streets living, life spinning  

All these sheeple, bah-ing and hating  

leave them all to the wolves  

Sheepdog is creating  

Glory fading  

my eternal flame, glows brighter than ever  

exsistential horrors very bitter  

cold taste of steel, Clever  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-02-01*
