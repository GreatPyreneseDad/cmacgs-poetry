# For the One

For the one I would do anything for  

Run the risk of living poor  

But rich in your love  

My soul basks in that glow  

Like a southbound train to Mexico  

Held down at the border of our union  

Like a train run off the tracks  

My fault no way back  

So in my reckless abandon halts  

A true story of loves lost meaning  

To give up something in loves last fleeting  

If it returns then truth in loves healing  

But if it falls away without taking  

A failed romance is not worth hating  

North wind will flow cold and abating  

In loves quest your soul in the making  

To be forged through hard weather  

Without this life would not be better  

So go hard into perilous water  

Grab the life by the collar  

Hold it tight and you will not fall  

But when you do gods angels call.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-01-09*
