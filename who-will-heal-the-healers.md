# Who Will Heal The Healers?

Who will heal the healers?  

The menders of the broken  

How all the people hurt  

How they plea  

And those who awnser the call  

Are they honored?  

The battered souls who hurt with you  

Who smell your blood and fear  

Or are you to deaf to hear?  

Who will heal the healers?  

And a voice called “i will”  

It was an artist  

A child  

A truth seeker  

A cherry tree on fire on the inside  

In full bloom upon a  

Snow covered winter feild  

when the child stood  

To say to the world  

“The healers will be healed  

when all sentient beings are free  

from the self imposed suffering we know all so well”  

The child screamed these words while The world listened  

And the healers wept  

For they knew the truth  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2017-03-18*
