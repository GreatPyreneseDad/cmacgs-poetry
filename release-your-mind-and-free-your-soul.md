# Release your mind and free your soul

Release yourself from expectation  

Cast your list of firsts aside  

Scratch out the blinding voice of lies  

An empty hand’s your invitation  

To become more than desire  

A want; a chain that binds your sighs  

The rope tide tight as you refuse  

Or choose, but drop your coy disguise  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-10-18*
