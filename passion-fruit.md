# Passion Fruit

Sweet silence Oh! Sweet flesh  

Sounds of sour mouths and  

Sweet tastes on tongues  

This Purple skin treasure  

Just tear a hole and suck out  

All the succulent juice  

If there where anything ever worth sharing  

Oh Passion fruit!  

Sing the beauty of her flavor  

Let its’ essence fill your nose  

Shall we take this moment  

To breathe deeper  

To smile at one another  

As we share this present pleasure  

Oh passion fruit ripen my love  

So I may be as delicious as  

You are.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-10-09*
