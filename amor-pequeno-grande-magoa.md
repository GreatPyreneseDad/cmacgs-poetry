# Amor Pequeno Grande Mágoa

When I write her poems in the morning she laughs and calls it  

“My morning boner feelings”  

I laugh because that’s funny but it’s not true.  

I feel this burning in my heart for her.  

Like I have never felt before  

Like a place I cannot leave  

Or rather won’t.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-05*
