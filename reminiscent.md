# Reminiscent 

To breathe fresh cool air  

A pedal down memory lane  

Sounds of cars and steel belts on roads winding like the infrared waves of heat that force you to wear shoes  

Seems like years ago  

But was just last winter  

El nino made it wet and warm  

You made it cold  

Long hair and well worn shoes  

Tan skin and a quit disposition  

El nino is gone now  

and so are you  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-07-27*
