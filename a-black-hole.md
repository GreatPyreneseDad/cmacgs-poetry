# A BLACK HOLE

I’ve think I remembered why it feels so good to be a child  

Those rich experiences of present moments  

We shall never feel more than then  

When we found ourselves without a past to anchor us in  

No painful memories to hold one down  

To float in weightless harmony  

Where truth was all we knew  

For the moment each one of us realized it was only lies  

It was too late  

Those rich feelings of life blanketed by memories of trauma  

Anger and the places we cannot stop telling others of  

Our excuses piled high above  

Those children of our own  

Can you remember what it is like for their sake  

Or simply your own piece of mind  

Can you find that hidden path to emerge on the other side  

Where maybe for only a moment you may feel that cool air  

A unique scent that wraps you in a warm blanket of energy  

Before that present tense takes you back to extinct moments  

Love would be so much easier if we would just live  

To be the one in the moment and not back three years  

Like a black hole  

a black hole  

memories  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-06-12*
