# All the lifetimes in my mind

Traveling in time  

Through my thoughts and imagination  

Infinite destinations  

Where will I take you today my dear?  

Surviving together in this isolation  

Left alone given time  

We will turn hell into heaven  

Where will you go today my dear?  

Another lifetime another year  

Traversing this time  

On the backs of our meditations  

And now we are here  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2017-09-27*
