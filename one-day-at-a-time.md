# One Day At A Time

Late at night I listened to the moon  

It grew tired as it viewed the earth turning in its sleep  

The sun light on her face  

Mother moon  

the goddess  

The love shared on a sandy beach at sunset  

Rose colored sky  

water like silk  

cloth in the wind  

A moment has passed into infinity  

The mother moon watches as Earth turns  

One day at a time  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2017-03-11*
