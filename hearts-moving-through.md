# Hearts moving through.

Found you in the park that day  

Sunday afternoon lets say…  

3pm I spotted you  

And by 5 we had plans that evening.  

I’m glad you fingered me i’d say  

An inside joke anyway  

Black dress tied up in back, you say it 80’s  

I like to call it pure sexy.  

Can we agree to disagree  

Microwave food and loose fitting clothes  

Flat tire on a jeep you don’t use  

Could be an adventure, to busy for you.  

Kristen does crossfit  

I cook good food  

If I live closer  

Grilled Cheese and tomato soup.  

I think you are beautiful  

You think I’m a fool  

Somedays hearts align  

Somedays they move through.

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-09-26*
