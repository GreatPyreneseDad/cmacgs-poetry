# Last thing is I love you

Said what I needed to  

Thats all i really  

ever wanted to tell you  

beautiful sunlight  

upon your wanderers dreams  

the last thing  

the very last thing  

that will survive long after  

our foot prints have been washed from this beach  

is the place thats been carved out of my heart  

the very last thing is  

i love you  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-25*
