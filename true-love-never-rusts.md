# True Love Never Rusts

I am rotating with the planet  

And now I’m dizzy.  

About to lose my head  

My thoughts are hazy.  

With so many people to love  

Why are we increasingly lonely?  

All the heart stones in the world  

Cannot displace the way I feel about you.  

That place where nothing but will do  

Why did I rip both our hearts out?  

That question makes me sick  

As I twiddle my fingers, quick flick.  

To the other side, where…  

I am gold in the moment,  

But even shining moments  

Rust with time.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-07-01*
