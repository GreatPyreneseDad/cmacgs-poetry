# A is for Acceptance

There are times I sit alone wondering  

What my life would be like if I could accept  

All the love I have been given in my life  

Rather than always running away from it  

I still think about if its too late  

I think about if i am still a person you could fall in love with  

At this point in my life I have strong doubts  

Not knowing if I can be whole again  

Or rather not believing in ghosts or miracles.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2014-03-06*
