# Golden One

what strange magic is this  

that has taken grip over my soul  

I watch a 3 second film roll  

on repeat for hours as time  

crept by me in 3 second  

bursts of infinity  

one moment of a break  

the light of pain  

then tugging your face  

the eyes  

what strange magic  

I am in a whirlpool  

in the deep atlantic of your eyes  

coral reefs like  

white polka dots  

have i ever  

wanted to be somewhere  

with all my soul  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-04*
