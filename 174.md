# A flowing diety

A little boy sees smoke billowing up behind the mountain  

Red beauty spilling forth from the lips of the earth  

Run or become geologic landmarks through times journey  

Wild sage burns  

Complements the midmorning breeze  

Coastal choke points bottleneck  

As land flows to push him into the sea  

With his ashes land claims what belongs to Poseidon  

Ghosts of many lay trapped beneath volcanic rock  

Ashes and souls enveloped in porous stone  

A reminder to all to listen to the calls  

Of our true mother  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-02-16*
