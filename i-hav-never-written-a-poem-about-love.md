# I hav never written a poem about love

I feel like I’ve never written  

A poem about love  

Only heartbreak or pain  

Then I remembered you were alive  

That simple thought changed the game  

My mind never connected before  

These beautiful dots orbiting  

Like treading water  

The harder I struggled  

The faster I drowned  

But once I was underwater  

My body was free  

Like the tables had turned  

The mind was now under the bodies decree  

Now my mind has awaken  

My thoughts start struggling  

But my body is still the master  

My heart bleeds to be free  

I don’t know if I can be happy  

I don’t know if you can see  

How I would trade my life  

So that other may breathe  

When my mind wakes from it’s rest  

It will be one with my body  

One part of the rest  

Like the wolf that howls until it loses it breath  

So the others may avoid capture  

So the others may avoid death  

For the young to live an breathe  

I feel like I’ve never written  

A poem about love  

Only heartbreak or pain  

Then I remembered you were alive  

Then I remembered you are still free  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-06-28*
