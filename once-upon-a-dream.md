# Once Upon A Dream

Wake up with her smell in my nose  Some kind of magic  

Some kind of love  

magnetic at a distance  

the ooposite of gravity  

the further i am the greater the pull on my heart  

wake up with a sense of longing  

its just our hearts sad that we have woken and interupted their lovers meeting in our dreams  

you touch me everytime i pass through your mind  

a sweetness i can not part with  

I would endure a thousand seperations from waking  

to exeperience one dream of your love.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-02*
