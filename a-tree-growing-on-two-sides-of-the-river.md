# A tree growing on two sides of the river

You chose the cold and I chose the rain  

The weather is what we crave  

The waves crashing upon the beach  

The sound of the door slamming in my face  

You left without saying goodbye  

I spent the rest of the year asking why  

A cold way to go  

A cold way to go  

A cold way to go  

But it seems we both enjoy the cold  

It seems we both enjoy the burn  

Is this what we deserve?  

I think we should turn around  

It’s no longer our world  

It belongs to her  

The one that we created  

The one that we love  

And so we must love each other  

To show her the warmth  

That exists in the cold  

In the rain  

And in Her homes  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-04-12*
