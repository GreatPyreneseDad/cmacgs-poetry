# Your Expression It’s Making Me Bleed

Your words are piercing me  

I feel your soul as you breath  

Beautiful sounds upon clouds of dreams.  

I fail to see the folly in not believing  

You are the one for me.  

Could call It obsession immaturely  

But in reality it is my infatuation  

With the words on the page  

More than words they strike a cord in me.  

I flee to sad yellow background,  

Words typing furiously, I plea  

With myself or maybe just subconsciously.  

I’ve found no solace in this poem only a message.  

Where have you come from this inspiration, this depth that I admire  

Like I was peasant and you royalty.  

Your expression it’s making me bleed.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-10-03*
