# Making love at the end of the world

Can we find something more  

More than lifestyles, all that allure  

The truth, jeweled by the cruel flow of  

Time –  

Waves of reality enveloping your soul  

Let us refute the petty wars of our fathers to  

Shake hands with gods and dissolve into the sky  

water drops form as your essence nucleates rain clouds; thunder rolls.  

Before us, the endless avenue of night stretches on into oblivion  

Release yourself from the fold  

before the heart sets on life like the sun on the day.  

Remove yourself from the cold  

Frost tipped lips can’t properly kiss  

Sun kissed skin can’t you see the warmth, more fire than wind  

dawn paints our backs as we become Mother Earth and Father Time making love at the end of the world.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-10-06*
