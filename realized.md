# Realized 

When you wake up and realize  

That everything you desired  

Everything you have possessed  

Means nothing  

Will you look out at the world and say  

“Enough”?  

Or will you look inside and say  

“More”  

Eternity exists in the small spaces  

The lengths of our inhalations  

And strength in our letting go.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2017-03-27*
