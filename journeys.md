# Journeys

Journeys are not called such for being easy.  

but they gain momentum  

the mornings are hard  

but once you get going  

then your heart is on fire  

happiness is everywhere  

when you are on a journey  

rich is the living you do  

experience colors your soul  

with the vibrancy of the universe  

the little pains of life melt  

away as if they where ice on an afternoon in an Austin summer  

and she is the sweet tea  

the one who pushed and then pulled you…soul first into the portal of adventure  

Journeys  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-07*
