# Maples

Sweet lips and maple leaves  

Smiles that send a crashing wave  

When every hill looks the same  

But we climb them all never finding  

That place that seemed so special  

Before the anguish and mistakes  

maple leaves and sunny days  

One day we all part ways  

There was life left in the forest  

Walking along the triliums path  

Unfounded memories of the aftermath  

The face of a God with the Devil’s wrath  

Maple leaves and sunny days  

When every hill I climb  

Couldn’t be long enough  

Fort nights of full moons  

Eternity in a moment  

Sweet lips and maple leaves  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-07-12*
