# Bipolar connection

Punk jacket, with studs gives you away  

Red lipstick so out of place  

Coffee and cigarettes  

The best breakfast you say  

Instantly I could tell  

But conversation gives it away  

You are bipolar  

Type one anyway  

I am type three, rapid cycling  

Up and down five times a day  

But I don’t deal with what you contain  

Stress levels rising, then they fade away  

I am a veteran, you never pay  

Your taxes to the man  

You are taxed heavily  

And receive his blessings one pill twice a day.  

I quit that train, hopped off now I’m sane  

I always was, but now i’m saved  

I can handle the ride and I want to say  

Keep your heart open, it never goes away.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-09-26*
