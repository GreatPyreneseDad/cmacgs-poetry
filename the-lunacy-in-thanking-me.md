# The Lunacy in Thanking Me

Why do you thank me for going to war?  

For the horror I’ve seen or the way we all deplore the lies of the season.  

You thank me on holiday or at anytime before, but November 11.  

That day that we are told to honor the sacrifice even more.  

But why is anyone thankful for war?  

Or thankful for suffering generated by false lies and profit schemes galore.  

Veteran the name of those witness to war, war of profit, blood covered money all over the floor.  

Swimming through propaganda we all start to mourn the loss of freedom. Militarized forces at home knocking down liberties door.  

Helpless now to defend a constitution in tatters.  

Tell me again why you thank me for going to war ?  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-11-10*
