# Ghosts

As my sight sinks deeper inward  

I feel a pressure on my third eye  

Then visions of ghosts in fields  

Each ghost a memory of suffering  

Echoes of agony silenced by explotions  

And the nonsense of a passing feeling  

Make believe people in a make believe world  

Come round to haunt our bodies and our souls  

As my sight sinks deeper inward  

I feel the karmic tide  

Then visions of a ghost in a porch seat  

This ghost a memory of home  

You have shown the light of your soul  

Now how can I hide  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2014-02-25*
