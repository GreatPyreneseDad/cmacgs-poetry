# Longest Day of the Year

It took seventeen years for the sun to set  

One ray at a time  

Across Bitterroots and valleys  

Warmth lasting through a short night  

We danced beneath moon beams and a full yellow crown sat upon your head in the photo you posed for  

Any place was here and anytime was now  

Eternity exists in the moments we share  

And within the moments  

time dissapears  

“How long does love last?” you asked me with that sarcastic edge  

Love is Infinite I said  

but…  

you already knew that  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-06-20*
