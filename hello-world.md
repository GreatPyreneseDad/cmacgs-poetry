# What this is all about.

There is no doubt that the world we live in is full of stimulus. Pain, pleasure, hot , cold, emotion, and how we interpret emotion. How can we even hope to express the pain or love we may feel, hold, share in our lives?  

For myself, the answer is poetry, that language which has programmed us, intertwined with our reality.  

Own the language, be the image, change the world.
