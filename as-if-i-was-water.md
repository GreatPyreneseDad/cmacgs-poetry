# As if I was Water

Sometimes when I feel like I am as a single leaf in the raging stream  

I like to imagine that I am the water, all around everywhere  

I feel as if I have a million lives  

Unable to be concentrated.  

You cannot concentrate water, as they say  

Incompressible for the day, happiness just a slow pool away  



If you could be , the rain and the fog  

We would mesh perfectly at the border  

I am eutectic and you are pure  

Vaporize me so I can fly  



Inside your cloud, Nucleation makes drops proud  

The white fluffy silhouette  

I am trapped in the beauty like a dream of nets  

I am a gilled fish, struggling, beating  



Alternating sinusoidal contactions  

Side to side my scales flake into the water  

I am as a single leaf again  

Trapped in the eternal spin  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-09-24*
