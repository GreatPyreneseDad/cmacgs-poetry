# these words are not my words

My WORDS are not MY words,  

ONLY my expression.  

I did not create them,  

words are ever present.  

So if you want to include my NAME,  

It really doesn’t matter.  

My words are not my words,  

Now self-interest begs to question…  

If your words were ONCE my words,  

then do we not SHARE expression?  

If these words are the only,  

the worlds eyes ever cover.  

Then let them see,  

my words are not my words,  

ONLY MY EXPRESSION.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-10-15*
