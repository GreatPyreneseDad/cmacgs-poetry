# Eudaimonia

Never was a long time ago  

And time doesnt matter so much Anymore  

Slow paced strolls on sandy isles  

Jade in your pocket  

Smooth green rocks among  

Jagged edges and a ruff sea  

Time doesn’t even exist here  

Only measured in the intervals  

Between waves crashing  

Moon and Sun as one body  

A light and its’ mirror  

Sun and Moon in a blue sky  

Eudaimonia look it up  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-08-24*
