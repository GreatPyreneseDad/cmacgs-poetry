# Nobody Picks Salal

Blackberry brambles and rose hips  

This never was an easy task  

Winding vines of thorns  

Vengeful horns and a tree frog  

Tucked into a rose blossom  

Black shining bubbles on bubble  

But nobody picks Salal  

Boring beautiful plant  

Resting among the shade  

The Evergreen giant  

The protectors squire  

The myceliums gift  

The berrys are bland  

Fill with worms sometimes  

No thorns these berries invite  

All to taste  

But nobody picks salal  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-08-02*
