# Vampire in My Dreams

Female philanderer, thief of youth  

Stealing hearts to feed  

Your selfish desire  

You think you have special powers  

Just a manipulative liar  

For strong hearts break  

But yours is like a black hole  

Energy vampire in my dreams  

Feeding on hope, love, self-esteem  

Your fangs of desire  

Using friendship as a disguise  

While you gently stalk your prize  

Thief of youth, female philanderer  

Stealing hearts to feed  

never satisfied, your words all lies  

as you burn me alive.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-10-22*
