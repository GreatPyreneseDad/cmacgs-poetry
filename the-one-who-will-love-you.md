# The One Who Will Love You

Love is easy if you love yourself  

But we are all so fucked up  

We try and pretend to be free  

When we are a slave to our fears  

“Noone is left for me”  

“Except for me”  

Repeat this ten times a day  

Then you will be free again  

Only then will the one  

Who will love you appear  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-02-10*
