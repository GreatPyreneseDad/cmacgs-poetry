# All This Shiny Shit

All this shiny shit  

It stinks up our reality  

It makes love requisite  

This shiny shit  

It brings no clarity  

I am free with out it  

All this shiny shit  

Lights in the sky  

Brighter than the light in our eye  

This shiny shit  

Clouds of whirling duality  

As we fade to darkness  

All this shiny shit.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-10-12*
