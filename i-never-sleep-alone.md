# I Never Sleep Alone

I do not often sleep  

when i am in love  

or when i am broken  

maybe they are one in the same  

sleepness nights laying up  

I never have trouble sleeping  

When lover is in my bed  

Warm hands and soft kisses  

placed gently atop my head  

Why do i complicate my life  

Simplicity could be paradise  

If i could wash out the poet  

Be like the one word man  

Bom dia menina  

how was your rest?  

Would you mind laying your head back down upon my chest?  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-24*
