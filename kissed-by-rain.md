# Kissed By Rain

Fill me with your light  

Oh beautiful morning  

Make me a small ray of sunlight  

To warm the one I love  

Fill me with your spirit  

A long deep sleep  

Crisp air that fills our lungs  

A love that will never leave  

It is in my heart  

A place that we can shelter  

When the weather turns cold  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-06*
