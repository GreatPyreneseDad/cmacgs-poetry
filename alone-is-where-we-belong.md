# Alone is where we belong

Brown eyed lover  

It was the way things would be  

Nothing more than a walk on the beach  

Another burned out lover  

Never going to see  

Takes more than a broken heart  

To see the love in a wanderer  

Memories and stains on the mind  

Just rain and an empty room  

What about all the trails you walked with me?  

Another girl who wants to trim  

Came to the land of the free  

Greens and scissors in her hands  

She only needs to be free  

Can’t love a bird and not see it fly  

Alone is where we belong  

Alone is where I find  

Me  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-12*
