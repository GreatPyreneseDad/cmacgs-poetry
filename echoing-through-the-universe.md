# Echoing Through The Universe

Bleeding out loud  

The thoughts of a heart  

Screaming out the window  

Into a soundless universe  

Bleeding out loud  

The hearts of the innocent  

Dreaming of the music  

In a soundless universe  

Bleeding out loud  

Red blood stained atmosphere  

Seeing the cycle collapse  

As we echo through the universe  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-10-24*
