# The Middle of Modernity

I sit in the crossroads of this life  

the middle of modernity and what I know to be right  

or what we all know as wrong but continue despite  

that painful feeling it causes  

is it your bodies afflictions or is it residing in your soul  

the place beyond life, the civilized humans toll  

to live amongst the wasteland and the hate  

inequity of each equals roll  

looking down or looking up we see the results of our economy  

the trash in the forest and the noise of what we want  

the global conspiracies and opulant jaunts across time zones  

pollute our airways and brainwaves with hate  

destroy  

what we have known as sacred  

destroy  

what we hold dear  

looking back you will think who are these people  

looking back you will say “what have I done?”  

looking back you will understand how much it really cost  

to have your modern life  

sitting at the crossroads of this life  

the middle of modernity and what we know to be right.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2014-11-19*
