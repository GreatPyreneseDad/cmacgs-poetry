# Filled up

Filled up my soul is floating  

Exhausted I am not  

When the body craves more  

I want to hold that close  

To my pounding heart  

Show me a smile  

So I know you are loved  

Filled up like a sunset sky  

On a late October afternoon  

I want to be the one  

But bury my desire  

Along with the longing  

I like to start the day  

Hungry  

Hungry for something  

Other than food  

Filled up like a texas sky  

in hurricane season  

Show me something taller than these hills  

Show me the mountains  

of your soul  

So i may climb into your heart  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-09*
