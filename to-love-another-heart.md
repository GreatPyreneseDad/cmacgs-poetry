# To Love Another Heart

They ride down waterfalls  

While my heart ruptures in two  

This sharp knife makes its way  

Through the memories we didn’t make  

This dragon this bird of longing  

The river of love  

Winding through eternity  

Cuting its way to its lover the ocean  

This stone on my heart  

Like the hoover damn  

Holding back my happiness  

Holding me captive inside  

My ears have been ringing  

The loud sound of treasonous leaders  

Finding no honor in serving their court  

To bow down to currency  

To stand in poverty  

For love I would  

For love of country I would still die  

To love another heart  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2014-03-17*
