# Prickly pears suck pretty hard damn

.  

If this is the last thing I see.  

This enormous view.  

Just short of the sea.  

Like a crescent shaped moon  

Over the wild and the free  

If this is the last thing I see  

The last burst of light into eternity  

I won’t fight , ill just be, but this place.  

Of amaranthine beauty, this place.  

Just short of the sea
