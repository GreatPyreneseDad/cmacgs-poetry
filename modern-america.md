# Modern America

Ferry man standing on a dry creek  

His tears are red  

a dry sand beach  

The rain ain’t come for a year and a week.  

Little boy cries on a dry porch seat  

His lips are chapped  

desert soldiers feet  

He cries out loud “there ain’t nothing to drink”  

Soapbox squak’r got no time for free  

His palms are greased  

his ass is cheap  

Sold the well and the heartland, then you and me.  

Veteran dying on the steps of liberty.  

His mind defined with visions of grief  

A doctor recommends psychotropics  

kills himself, the drugs were free.  

Citizen standing in the choosers box  

ink blue pen  

ready to vote impeach  

who to choose next  

the prospects bleak.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-11-24*
