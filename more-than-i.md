# More than I

I’m gonna try and move to crawl  

not sure if I have what it takes  

the hot sand shifts as it bakes  

I am not quite certain how I fall  

But what I do know  

Of love and war, is abstract, far before  

In a world I only know now in dreams  

for what it seems, I can choose to believe.  

I am feeling crushed and its not my fault  

Seeing a mask, seeing a hand.  

I am the mask now  

And the hand, it is my hand.  

Postion of the knife vital.  

Piercing nervous tissue more than  

A softtissue scar.  

my greatist fear manifests from before.  

I ripped my heart out with my k-bar,  

but it wasnt me, but those I love.  

Who pulled it out.  

The concerned crowd now black and white  

Blood  

Locked in, invested, in this fight.  

I bleed blood, I bleed bright.  

To fill my brothers with hope tonight.  

The slayed mans memory, is not forthright.  

So how can we trust anything of antiquity?  

Was it a genuine attempt, or insanity?  

The machine in place more than gears.  

Culture instills fear, to change unknown.  

Rearranged again in hope, delayed by what?  

Like a psuedogene,  

deteriorated after it was duplicated.  

If instibility exists it will be challenged.  

And as a being of earth we are inherently  

As stable as we can be, but of what force?  

Force of mind, it binds the heart, and twists the mind.  

A single footstep recorded in time.  

To say that is a blatant lie, I lied.  

In wait to tell you why.  

The footstep is recorded in mind.  

And brought back on borrowed hides.  

To fill our country with uttmost pride.  

If this helps you know why.  

There is so much hurt here, trapped inside.  

Then it could be a good reason to try.  

Learning real ways to be kind.

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-03-15*
