# Who Are We?

who are we  

to tell ourselves  

that it is all going to be ok  

who are we  

to claim victory  

when we know what we came from  

who are we  

to hide away  

while we watch the looters  

the one who sold our sons  

into blood baths and emotion  

while we watched  

who are we  

to not demand justice  

while our country bleeds  

who are we?  

in this land of the free  

who are we…  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2014-10-19*
