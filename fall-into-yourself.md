# Fall into yourself

Through that doorway of love  

Fall  

So you have no chance to turn back  

Fall into  

Yourself  

Where all your love exists  

Fall  

Down deep into the light  

Within you is a resevoir  

Where you may drink until  

Your heart is overflowing  

Fall  

So others may follow  

Into yourself  

Go  

Further  

So others may follow  

So you may create the space  

Where none exists  

Fall into yourself  

And never come back  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-11-01*
