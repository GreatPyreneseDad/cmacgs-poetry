# Caught in a Wave

caught in a wave  
i've never felt more free  
like i was remembering  
a weightlessness  

spinning  

the world is grains of sand  
and seawater in solution  
a solution to my worldly fears  

it's not drowning  
if you refused to breathe  
when your body shouted for air  

summersaults and vortex  

and when i surface  
the first breath feels like  
it came too soon  

and i'm here again  

the shore like some foreign land  
where i am not welcome  

---

*Christopher MacGregor*
