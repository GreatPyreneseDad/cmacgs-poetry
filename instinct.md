# Instinct

I feel predatory  

twilight beacons me  

shed the chains  

societies expectations  

hunt with keen senses  

avoid being prey  

Belief is an illusion  

created by wanting minds  

building your false realities  

to impart upon others  

Living is this artificial construct  

funneling basic instinct  

into a mathematic function  

f(people) equals neurotic behavior  

equals  

You, standing beauty before my crystal irises  

electrical impulse through rhodopsin  

your hair, my pulse, I spin  

how can we make it .  

so we all win?  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-03-03*
