# One word Ana

Red velvet would have suited her  

Tan skin on a naive beauty  

Let’s not joke or tease  

It’s just business  

It’s just life  

Banana splits at 3 am perhaps  

Another day  

Perhaps another year  

Long smile long ear  

A longing of the heart  

Of the road  

One word Ana  

Walks down market street  

A memory in a sea of thoughts  

A dream in a pile of words  

A banana split at 3 am  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-03-27*
