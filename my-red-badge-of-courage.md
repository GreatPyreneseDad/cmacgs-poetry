# My red badge of courage

Kirkuk Iraq  





























Stuck on an island  

So close to the future  

But we won’t live that long anyway  

Our children may suffer the same fate.  

A surgery can save a life  

But not the family  

Paralyzed from the neck down  

Jaquez took his own life naturally.  

I, third generation in this blessed place  

Journey to the war zone only to face  

The demons I did not know, forgiven in peace  

My red badge of courage is visually.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-02-01*
