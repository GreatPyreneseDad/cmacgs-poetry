# Lost soul

Beat down warn thin walking shoes  

Weathered leather and old jeans  

Dirt path no map off trail hike  

Dirt-less golden fungus emerging from moss  

Ocean crashing sounds echo off trees  

Two hundred foot tall giants  

Sun beams cut paths to the forests floor  

Old pocket knife in hand  

the sounds of dog collars in ear  

Nothing finds it’s way home  

Like a lost soul  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-10-25*
