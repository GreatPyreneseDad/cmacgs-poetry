# Waiting on Winter

The cold is my brother  

He holds me like family  

Waiting for him to come  

to keep me as company  

The familiar alone comforts us  

The crisp crunch of frozen  

Water isn’t wet and warmth  

Comes from the inside out  

Growth is more than saying  

You undertand  

Forgiveness more than  

Letting go  

Waiting on winter to wash this soul  

Waiting for a cold place to rest my heart  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-10-29*
