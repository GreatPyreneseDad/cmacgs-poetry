# Lady Freedom on trial for blasphemy

Packing up the planet  

In neatly labeled plastic bags  

Reaching for stars on stilts  

While others burn ancestors bones  

Stones, I’m stoned, everyone’s alone  

The piggies are back in the pantry  

Jokes, I joke, everybody is broke  

Lady freedom is burning in Rome.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-06-15*
