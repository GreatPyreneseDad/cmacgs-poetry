# Clay Bowl

Cute town boulevards  

You picked a good spot  

Back behind the drag  

Nice courtyard sitting  

Just to be so close again  

Fills me up with the love i am  

I hope you can feel it  

Just a little bit of whats there  

It will always be there for you  

I will fill my self so full  

that love will be spilling from the edges of my clay bowl  

come and drink  

my love

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-26*
