# Jelly

at this moment my soul is hurting  

i feel the call of death summoning me  

i want to walk towards the sound  

but someone has tied me to the ground  

I cant move  

seeing everyone walk past me  

calling out to my brothers  

they keep walking because if they dont  

they stand the risk of being crushed  

but nothing is coming to crush me  

i am forced to watch as all those ahead  

are forced to choose between falling off a cliff  

or being smushed like a wine grape  

i can see them poping, screaming  

then the brave ones jump off the cliff “puff”  

puff puff puff, little sounds of flesh hitting earth  

then i am alone, with my last memories of life  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2011-12-15*
