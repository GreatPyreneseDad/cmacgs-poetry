# Free As A Bird

You dance, it is beautiful  

I write poetry.  

Your pictures left this whole in me.  

Shining light in this dark world.  

A swift pirouette, blonde hair spinning around, beautiful girl  

I fail to breathe, while your beauty astounds.  

I’m like a fish out of water,  

trying to understand what it is like to be as free as a bird.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-09-22*
