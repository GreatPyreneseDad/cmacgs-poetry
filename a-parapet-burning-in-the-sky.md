# A Parapet Burning In The Sky

This feeling like I’m alone  

Release myself from cold stone  

Prison of your love or empty chasm  

This feeling like it never happened  

The light is in your heart  

The darkness in your lies  

And for some silly reason  

I thought I found myself in your eyes  

It doesn’t mean much  

No big surprise  

There is no truth in love  

Or grace in the ways we hide  

Our hearts have been abandon  

By our souls, a moral low tide  

The parapet is on fire  

In this desert of my mind.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-10-22*
