# Tears from my Brothers eyes

Blatant but bashful tears flow  

From the eyes of my brother  

His blonde hair and blue eyes  

Remind him of his mother as he gazes  

Into his own reflection  

Introspection is followed by guilt  

Followed by self loathing  

Suicidal uttering  

His reflection reminds him of his mother  

He says while raping in the shower  

Love left him the day his mother did  

She left his heart wounded and raw  

His biggest worry is that he will never experience that again.  

Without a clear bearing  

He launches into his young life loveless for now.  

But I am not worried he is strong.  

And he is loved.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-01-03*
