# Who Are You

Who reads my poems  

Anonymous footprints across my sanctuary  

I wrote them all for you  

So one day  

My heart could be dissected  

In my absence  

But you have come to soon  

And I am curious to know  

Who are you?  

To walk into my holy place  

To walk into my heart  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2019-06-26*
