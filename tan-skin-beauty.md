# Tan Skin Beauty

Tan skin beauty  

How can we ever be the same  

When every ray of sunlight  

Changes us  

Hold on to those moments  

The ones we spend in paradise  

You may search your whole life  

Never to find that same beach  

Where you left your heart  

Tan skin beauty  

The golden one  

How can we ever be the same  

When every smile from you  

Sends waves of love through me  

Hold on to these moments  

The ones that feel like eternity  

You may search your whole life  

Never to find that same smile  

That has broken you open  

The one that has set you free  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-02-12*
