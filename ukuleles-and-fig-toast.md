# Ukuleles and Fig Toast

This fog, the smoke, your breathe  

I know  

We moved on two wheels sure  

fast as legs go  

Ukuleles and fig toast  

Bouncing back from coast to coast  

Mushroom hikes are all we know  

Camping on this forest road  

Campfires and toadstools  

Cracking backs and taking notes  

Leaving cities and paved roads  

Now you know  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-10-27*
