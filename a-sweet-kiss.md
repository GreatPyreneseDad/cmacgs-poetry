# A sweet kiss

Imagine that the world is free from pain  

What would we feel then  

Would it be like a cold summer rain  

wet alligator tears from the sky  

She wept for the loss of experience  

Who becomes and who succumbs  

Where is the meaning in a  

Painless reality  

Imagine that the world was free from pain  

Could joy or happiness exists  

Without pain could there be bliss  

Wet alligator tears from her eyes
