# On a River

These are the best memories  

When wind flowing through leaves  

This memory the best memory  

Of water flowing past the trees  

Sky is the limit  

On this memory  

It does not match this one  

But flows from moon to sun  

What does your heart say to the river  

Does it bleed for the bluegill or the heron  

For if you have a bias you miss the river  

Intertwined as the vines of a melon patch  

It flows through dams and around  cities  

Blue green brown beauty  

Kissing the land with your moist lips  

Rinsing the sin to the sea  

Where it will sink through eternity  

Joining the deep ocean of sin  

Poseidon watches with a weeping brow  

as his kingdom crumbles he cannot win.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2012-01-12*
