# My Loo

My Love since we danced  

My soul has been restless  

To be a spirit on the wind  

Even for a moment  

Is better than a thousand lifetimes in a cage.  

The lover and the moon  

The river and the leaf  

To float again  

To be free  

My love since we danced  

I will never be the same  

All my thoughts are tipped  

With the sweetness of your name and breath upon my ear.  

To be a shining light  

Even for a moment  

Is better than an eternity  

In the clutches of darkness  

The tower and the prince  

The great adventure  

To love again  

To be free  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-12-21*
