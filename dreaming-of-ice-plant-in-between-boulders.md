# Dreaming of Ice Plant In-between Boulders

Ice plant in between boulders  

Stacked on lands edge with the sea  

Travelled here with the notion  

Man can control more than himself  

If you dream then dream devotion  

If you see then see the dream  

Every concept just a notion  

There is more to life than you and Me.  

Ice plant in between boulders  

Every concept just a dream  

Travelled here with the notion  

That only you can set you free.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2013-10-19*
