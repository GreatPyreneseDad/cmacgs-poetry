# The heart is a place of many dimensions

The heart is a place of many dimensions.  

How many heartbreaks can a heart take?  

Before the feeling don’t go away anymore  

I fell onto the floor and my blood left me  

Now i am less than a man  

Now i am but time within the sands  

Before the feelings wouldn’t leave  

I had blue eyes and a wide grin  

The heart is this place of many dimensions  

You found me in the dark pit  

Upside down and trying to dig  

My eyes are grey and to smile is sin  

Am i the beloved or the helpless pig  

Wallowing in the mud of self pity  

before the feelings wouldn’t leave
