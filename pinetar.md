# Pinetar

What measure of madness  

My Shams has come and gone  

My books have been burned  

Upon a lovers flame  

No comfortable life could suit me  

Not with a burning heart  

A flame set upon heart sized blobs of pinetar and ripped up pages of poems that mean nothing to me since my eternity has come and gone.  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2016-01-22*
