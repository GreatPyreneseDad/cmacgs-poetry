# familiar but far

I cut myself on your smile and embrace  

calling me “Baby” was like a scapel run across my soft skin  

only to fall open once you lifted your hands from my heart  

now each text message  

is cause for  

the sutures i’ve placed to fail  

my heart pushing out my  

battered soul into the cold air  

each time i reply i feel you  

moving further away from me  

slowly cautiosly  

like i am a wild animal injured  

and dangerous  

but i am more like a cloud  

and your distance is the change from day to night  

where i have been reabsorbed  

into the air of humanity  

only a memory of a sky  

Darkened by rain and grey  

to become clear and bright  

come the rise of the sun  

---

*Christopher MacGregor — 2015-12-28*
